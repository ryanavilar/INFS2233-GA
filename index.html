Who is Lola Collective?

Well, since you asked…

At Lola Collective, we seek to discover Brisbane-based emerging artists, whose pieces are inspired and bursting with individuality and soul. We are dedicated to connecting passionate artists and art aficionados alike, and expand the breadth of talent and eccentricity of, the Brisbane art scene.

Our Office



35 Charteris street, Paddington

Next-Business-Day Delivery

Expensive shipping costs and long wait-times are a thing of the past. If you reside within the red delivery zone shown on the map, enjoy the luxury of next-business day delivery right to your front door! Did we mention, delivery is free?

Pick up from Lola Collective HQ

If you can’t wait to get your hands on your brand-spanking-new art work, or want to pop in to see us, you are more than welcome to pick up your purchase, directly from us! Our opening hours are 8:30 til 5pm Monday to Friday.

Freight and Shipping

If you do not fall into our delivery zone and are unable to pick up your purchase, do not fear! The team at Lola Collective are more than happy to organise freight and shipping to your doorstep. To enquire about our freight and shipping costs, drop us a line on (07) 3210 0123 or email orders@lolacollective.com.au
